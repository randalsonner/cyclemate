about

Urban cycling can be a rush, but you can't always rely on raw intuition to know the safest routes to navigate within a busy city. CycleMate can be used during a ride to save the bike route you took, for others to experience using turn-by-turn voice-over navigation with their GPS enabled phone. This app will connect urban riders, and will enable people to experience what others have before, in a fun and interactive way.

features

find page

Use this feature to find routes that start near your current location, or type in a known route number to be navigated to the start of a specific one.

record page

This feature uses your GPS enabled smartphone to track the ride you take, and can be shared with other riders to experience for themselves.

profile page

The profile page will give you up-to-date data on the routes you have taken and the ones you have posted.

technology

Using the Google Maps API, CycleMate seamlessly works with your GPS enabled smartphone to determine your current location and record the rides you take. CycleMate would not be able to perform at the level it does, without the advanced technology that Google Maps has to offer, and we are delighted to parter with them on this product.
